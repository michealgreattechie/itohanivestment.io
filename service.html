Our Services

At Itohan Investment Limited, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to help individuals, businesses, and organizations achieve sustainable growth. Our multidisciplinary expertise allows us to deliver practical, efficient, and innovative solutions across diverse sectors.

Tax Consultancy

Navigating complex tax regulations can be challenging. Our seasoned tax consultants provide professional advice on tax planning, filing, and compliance, helping clients reduce liabilities while staying fully aligned with national and international tax laws.

Import and Export Services

With a deep understanding of international trade protocols, we simplify the process of moving goods across borders. From customs documentation and clearance to freight coordination and supply chain support, we ensure seamless transactions and timely delivery.

Construction and Engineering

Our construction arm is built on a commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability. We undertake residential, commercial, and infrastructural projects, delivering structures that meet global standards with precision and technical excellence.

Real Estate Development

We develop and manage real estate assets that deliver both value and comfort. Our services include property acquisition, development, leasing, and facility management, providing end-to-end solutions tailored to your needs.

Transportation & Logistics

Efficiency and reliability define our transport operations. We offer passenger and goods transportation, corporate mobility solutions, and logistics management with a fleet of well-maintained vehicles and experienced drivers.

Investment Advisory

We provide strategic consultancy and investment advisory services to help businesses identify opportunities, manage risks, and achieve measurable growth. Our team leverages market insight and financial expertise for sound decision-making.
